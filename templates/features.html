📝 Journaling

Write freely with a powerful editor that adapts to your style.

Editor Options
  • MyST Markdown - Write in markdown with live preview
  • Rich Text (Quill) - WYSIWYG editor for visual formatting
  • Image Support - Paste or drag images directly into entries
  • Auto-Save - Never lose your work with automatic draft saving
Entry Metadata
  • Mood Tracking - Tag entries with your mood (Ecstatic, Happy, Neutral, Sad, Angry)
  • Energy Level - Track your energy (High, Medium, Low)
  • Location - Add your city for weather correlation
  • Custom Tags - Organize entries with your own tags
🔒 Encrypted by Default: All journal entries are encrypted at rest. Only you can read your entries.

💬 POV (Point of View) Sharing

Share specific thoughts with friends without sharing your entire entry.

How to Share a POV

Use the POV button in the editor toolbar, or type the syntax manually:

Today was an amazing day! I went to the beach.

{pov} @alice @bob
Just wanted to share - saw the most beautiful sunset today!
Reminded me of when we all went camping last year.
{/pov}

The rest of my entry stays private...
✅ Key Features
  • Share paragraphs, not entire entries
  • Friends only - must be connected first
  • Auto-appears in recipient's journal for that date
  • Recipients can reply, creating conversations
  • Email notifications when you receive a POV
⚠️ Good to Know
  • You cannot unsend a POV once shared
  • Recipients can delete POVs from their journal
  • Think before you share!

🔒 Privacy & Encryption

Your thoughts are safe. Here's exactly how we protect your most private writing.
🔐 Encryption at Rest
  • Per-User Encryption - Each user has unique encryption keys
  • AES Encryption - Industry-standard Fernet encryption
  • Database Admins Can't Read - Even with database access
  • Backups Encrypted - Data safe even in backups
🤖 How AI Works with Encryption
  1. Entry temporarily decrypted in memory
  2. AI analyzes and generates scores
  3. Only scores saved (not your text)
  4. Entry re-encrypted and stored
  5. Decrypted version never touches disk
🌐 Multi-Tenant Isolation

Complete data isolation between users. Your data is never mixed with anyone else's, and all queries are filtered by your user account.

🔒 HTTPS/TLS

All data transmitted over secure connections. Session data protected with secure cookies.
